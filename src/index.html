What's Crypt?

From the Greek Kryptos, it means 'Hidden.'

It has represented, through time, a way to send highly important information from governments, countries or just people eachother.


The first known use of a modern cipher was by
Julius Caesar (100 b.C.)
Who did not trusted his messengers when communicating with his governors and officers.
